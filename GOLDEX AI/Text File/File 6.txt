## **🛠️ TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **❌ Problem: EA Not Starting**
**Solution:**
1. Check **"Auto Trading"** button is green
2. Right-click chart → **"Expert Advisors → Allow live trading"**
3. Check **"Experts"** tab for error messages

### **❌ Problem: "Not enough money"**
**Solution:**
1. Reduce **MaxLotSize** to `0.01`
2. Reduce **MaxRiskPercent** to `1.0`
3. Check your account balance

### **❌ Problem: No Signals**
**Solution:**
1. Make sure **EnableTestMode** is `true`
2. Check XAUUSD market is open
3. Wait 30 seconds for first signal

### **❌ Problem: Connection Lost**
**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Re-login to Coinexx account
3. Restart MT5
